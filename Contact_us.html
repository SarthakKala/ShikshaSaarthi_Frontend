Frequently asked questions

What is Shiksha Saarthi?

Shiksha Saarthi is a web-based platform dedicated to enhancing the quality of education in rural areas. It combines educational resources with personalized mentorship to help rural students excel academically and develop essential life skills.

Who can use ShikshaSaarthi?

It is designed for rural students, teachers, mentors, and parents who seek to improve the quality of education in rural areas.

Is ShikshaSaarthi free to use?

Yes, ShikshaSaarthi is free to use. We believe in providing equal access to education for all, especially in rural communities.

Are there materials available for different grade levels and subjects?

Absolutely. We provide resources for various grade levels and subjects to cater to a diverse range of educational needs.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues on the platform?

f you encounter technical issues, please reach out to our technical support team for assistance. We are here to help you resolve any problems you may encounter while using ShikshaSaarthi.

How can I access digital literacy training?

You can access digital literacy training modules through our platform. These modules are designed to enhance your digital skills

Is the content customized for rural students?

Yes, our curriculum is customized to meet the specific needs and challenges faced by rural learners, ensuring relevance and effectiveness

How do I become a mentor on ShikshaSaarthi

To become a mentor, you can sign up as a mentor on our platform and provide your qualifications and areas of expertise. Our matching algorithm will connect you with students who can benefit from your mentorship.
