Dr. Hargobind Khurana Scholarship Scheme:

  • This scholarship is offered to students belonging to economically weaker sections of society for pursuing higher education
  • Key points: Financial assistance for tuition fees, books, and other educational expenses.
  • Application: Apply through the Department of Welfare of Scheduled Castes, Backward Classes & Minorities, Government of Punjab.
